Meet the Team

Phil the Philodendron - This easy-going vine loves to climb and enjoys moderate watering. He's always up for a chat (well, mostly listening) and adds a touch of jungle vibes to any room.

Aloe the Aloe Vera - This spiky superstar is a natural healer with soothing properties. He's low-maintenance and thrives in bright, indirect light. Aloe is always there to lend a hand (or rather, a leaf)!

Sansevieria the Snake Plant - This low-light loving champion is practically unkillable. Sansevieria is the ultimate air purifier and brings a touch of architectural style to any space.

Violet the African Violet - This vibrant bloomer brings a pop of color to any room. Violet requires a bit more care with watering and likes bright, but filtered light. But when she blooms, it's worth the effort!
